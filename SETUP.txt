SEC Service Configurator — email orders
======================================

1. Open js/config.js
2. Sign up at https://formspree.io and create a form.
3. Replace YOUR_FORM_ID in FORMSPREE_ACTION with your form path (e.g. https://formspree.io/f/abcdefgh).
4. Formspree will email submissions to the address you verify on their dashboard.

Open the site locally:  python3 -m http.server 8765
Then visit http://localhost:8765/

Deploy: upload the sec-service-configurator folder to Netlify, GitHub Pages, or any static host.
